Origins

Origins Are Traits To Have That Positively Or Negatively Effect Gameplay Based On The Origin

Human

  • No special abilities or traits.
  • Play Minecraft normally.

Avian

  • Tailwind: You are quicker on foot and sprint more efficiently.
  • Featherweight: You fall as gently as a feather.
  • Vegetarian: Restricted to vegetables; cannot eat meat.
  • Fresh Air: Beds need to be at an altitude of at least 151 blocks.

Arachnid

  • Webbing: Trap enemies in cobwebs (13s cooldown).
  • Web Cocoon: Damage mitigation while in cobwebs.
  • Climbing: Climb solid blocks by holding jump.
  • Carnivore: Restricted to a meat-based diet.

Elytrian

  • Gift of the Winds: Propel into the air (30s cooldown).
  • Winged: Permanent Elytra wings.
  • Need for Mobility: Heavy armor slows you down.
  • Claustrophobia: Weakness in low ceilings.
  • Brittle Bones: Take more fall damage.
  • Aerial Combatant: Deal more damage while gliding.

Shulk

  • Hoarder: Extra inventory slots kept upon death.
  • Sturdy Skin: 20% damage reduction without armor.
  • Large Appetite: Hunger depletes faster.
  • Unwieldy: Shields break instantly when used.
  • Strong Arms: Break stone without a pickaxe.

Feline

  • Strong Ankles: No fall damage.
  • Acrobatics: Higher jumps while sprinting.
  • Nine Lives: 1 less heart of health.
  • Nocturnal: Gain strength and night vision in low light.
  • Weak Arms: Slower mining without strength.
  • Catlike Appearance: Creepers are scared of you.

Enderian

  • Teleportation: Teleport to blocks (short cooldown).
  • Scared of Gourds: Weakness with pumpkins in inventory.
  • Familiar Face: Endermen won’t attack unless provoked.
  • Hydrophobia: Take damage from water.

Merling

  • Gills: Breathe underwater but not on land.
  • Aqua Affinity: Break blocks underwater as on land.
  • Sea Inhabitant: Passive to underwater mobs.
  • Like Water: Float and sink at will.
  • Ocean’s Gift: Immune to trident damage.

Blazeborn

  • Impervious: Restore health and increase damage after fire, magma, or lava damage.
  • Nether Inhabitant: Spawn naturally in the Nether.
  • Ember: Ignite mobs when on fire (3s cooldown).
  • Hotblooded: Immune to poison and hunger effects.
  • Hydrophobia: Take damage from water.

Phantom

  • Phantomize: Enter spectator mode temporarily.
  • Spiritual Body: Traverse through blocks and become invisible in spectator mode.
  • Undead: Burn in daylight unless invisible or wearing a helmet.

Fox

  • Camouflage: Blend with the environment to avoid mob attacks.
  • Fast-footed: Increased speed and smaller size.
  • Pounce: Charge and leap in the direction you're looking.
  • Berry Craver: Sweet berries restore more hunger and sometimes grant regeneration.
  • Smaller Heart: Maximum health reduced by 4 hearts.
  • Fast Metabolism: Get hungry faster.
  • Strong Ankles: No fall damage.

Slimecican

  • Bouncy Body: Reduce fall damage due to a bouncy body.
  • Super Jump: Jump higher, dealing damage upon landing (3s cooldown).
  • Sticky: Slow melee attackers and reduce their damage.
  • Fragmentation: Shrink in size as health decreases.
  • Recovery: Consume slime balls to heal.

Inchling

  • Nimble: Immune to thorns and velocity-based damage.
  • Hyper-active: Consume sugar for a speed boost.
  • Small Appetite: Require less food, sugar provides lasting saturation.
  • Bite-sized: Smaller size with 5 less hearts.
  • Poisonous: Inflict fatal poison on critical hits (7s cooldown).

Bee

  • Fatality: Deal 50% more damage to poisoned targets.
  • Stingers: Use stingers for poison attacks; perish when all are used.
  • Pollenate: Recover stingers by sneaking on flowers (breaks the flower).
  • Diurnal: Weakened and slowed at night or in rain.
  • Short Lifespan: 3 fewer hearts than humans.

Starborne

  • Stress: Explode when stress fills up; faster cooldowns at 70% stress.
  • Meditate: Reduce stress by sneaking at full health.
  • Cosmic Gift: Jump higher and take no fall damage.
  • Hyper Leap: Propel in a direction and launch entities upward (cooldown).
  • Shooting Star: Fire damaging projectiles (cooldown).
  • Nonviolent: Chance of immobilization when harmed.
  • Cold Vacuum: Take double fire damage.

Piglin

  • Pride: Carry gold to reduce incoming damage (up to 90%).
  • Boasting Firepower: Melee deals bonus damage with nearby entities.
  • Gluttony: Increased hunger when healing.
  • Courage: Immune to weakness and wither effects.

Elven

  • Lifeweaver: Regenerate health and gain absorption based on recent damage.
  • Endless Quiver: Automatically generate arrows when holding a bow.
  • Imbue: Enhanced projectiles deal magic damage to undead.
  • Swift: Increased movement speed for effortless traversal.
  • Permeable: Take 10% more damage from all sources.

Voidwalker

  • Beelzebub: Deal bonus damage based on missing health.
  • Soulburst: Consume stacks of Beelzebub to deal extra damage.
  • Umbral Veil: Gain speed and avoid projectiles while sprinting.
  • Sinister Aura: Frighten passive mobs, making them flee.
  • Life Drain: Reduce health when damaging targets; stops at 1 health.

Diviner

  • Prescience: Share max health increase with another player.
  • Oracle: Transfer damage and healing to the bonded player.
  • Aegis: Reflect 50% of damage back to attackers.
  • Divine Aura: Bonded players glow and can be seen through walls.
  • Fragility: More susceptible to damage at lower health.
  • Instability: Unique status effects reduce max health (down to 1).
