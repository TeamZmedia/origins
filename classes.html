Classes

Classes Are Chosen After Your Origin For Another Buff

Nitwit

  • No additional perks.
  • Continue without further abilities.

Archer

  • Agility: Move faster while drawing a bow or crossbow.
  • Precision: Arrows travel straight without gravity.

Beastmaster

  • Fauna Friends: Tamed animals gain permanent health buffs.
  • Effective Empathy: Potion effects you have apply to your tamed animals.

Blacksmith

  • Great Craftsmanship: Crafted equipment has better durability, efficiency, and damage.
  • Efficient Repairs: Crafted equipment restores more durability during repairs.

Cleric

  • Proficient Brewing: Extended potion effects last even longer with redstone.
  • Potent Brews: Potions brewed with glowstone are more amplified.

Cook

  • Good Meals: Cooked food grants additional saturation.
  • Rejuvenating Soup: Stews provide regeneration effects.

Explorer

  • Fit: Sprinting does not consume hunger.
  • Explorer Kit: Start with a compass, clock, and nine death-proof maps.

Farmer

  • Green Thumb: Crops may grow instantly when planted.
  • Bountiful Harvest: Sometimes harvest double the yield from crops.

Lumberjack

  • Tree Capitator: Cut down an entire tree using an axe.
  • Replanting: Logs may drop saplings of the same type when broken.

Merchant

  • Great Deals: Receive discounts when trading with villagers.
  • Charisma: Wandering Traders are more likely to visit.

Miner

  • Vein Mine: Instantly mine ores and adjacent ores with a pickaxe.
  • Veteran Excavator: Mining does not consume hunger.

Rancher

  • More Births: Animals breed more offspring when nearby.
  • Scavenging: Gain extra materials from killing mobs.

Rogue

  • Sneaky: Your nameplate remains invisible, even when not sneaking.
  • In the Shadows: Deal more damage while invisible.

Warrior

  • Shield Wield: Gain extra protection when blocking with a shield.
  • Wrath Bloodlust: Gain strength as health decreases.
